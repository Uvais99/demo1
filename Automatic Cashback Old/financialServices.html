How card-linked marketing works

  • Consumers pay with a linked card

    Depending on the program structure, a consumer's card can be auto-enrolled through their bank or consumers can manually link any card in their wallet into the program.

  • Notifications inform consumers of the cash-back earned

    After making a purchase at a participating advertiser, in-store or online, consumers receive a cash-back notification.

  • Consumers receive cash back or reward currency

    Consumers get cash back directly to their enrolled card via a statement credit or loyalty currency.
