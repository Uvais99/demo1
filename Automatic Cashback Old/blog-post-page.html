April 27, 2021

Cheese card partners with Mycashpays

Written By
John Doe

We’re excited to formally welcome Cheese, a revolutionary digital banking and social cause platform, to Mycashpays!

Through our partnership, Cheese users can earn cash back while shopping at over 10,000 of their favorite retailers and restaurants using either a physical or virtual debit card. Cheese is on a mission to affect change and empower individuals with financial freedom, primarily serving Asian-Americans and immigrants to the US. The company is focused on providing accessible, zero feed rewards-based banking, and we’re happy to play a role in supporting their cause.

"By offering our customers the opportunity to earn cash back rewards on Mycashpays vast network of brands, we’re helping them to save money, which ties into our mission to empower people with financial freedom. Our partnership also allows for customers to support especially Asian businesses, affected by the pandemic, as part of our focus as a social cause platform,” said Ken Lian, CEO, and co-founder of Cheese.

The Mycashpays team is proud to be a part of Cheese’s efforts in supporting Asian-American and immigrant-owned businesses, charitable organizations, and at-risk communities through their platform, which contributes a portion of each purchase to its charitable Giveback Fund.

With every purchase made through Cheese, customers have the option to donate any amount of their rewards, including the cash back they earn with Mycashpays.

"As a mission-driven business, it’s our goal to find ways to put money back in people’s wallets when they spend on everyday purchases," said Ryan Wuerch, CEO and founder of Mycashpays. "We are proud to be supporting Cheese customers by offering access to cash back from more than 10,000 brands and retailers.”
