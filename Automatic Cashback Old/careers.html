Product Managers :

Are you excited about building products that will be used by millions of Indians? Do you love solving real technology problems of scalability, security, and world-class products? Do you have the passion to work in a fast-paced environment where hustle and hunger to learn more, do more, achieve more and #GetMore, is the key to unparalleled growth?

Then MCP.com has a super exciting opportunity for you in Product Management.

We are looking for individuals who are passionate about working on and managing high-scale and robust products. With MCP's recent $10mn fundraise, we are looking to grow 10x. This can be a great career move to accelerate your own growth.

Roles and responsibilities of this role would include the below:

  • Work with Cross-functional teams on defining the strategies, prioritize and define the Product roadmap & growth features for Mobile Apps/Web and other consumer facing platforms.
  • Set the vision for the product, clearly define metrics, reaching alignment across business, product and engineering team members and stakeholders, and of course "rock star" execution.
  • Adopt a data-driven approach to understand the user segment, to identify new opportunities and pain points, set up a new process or improve the existing one.
  • Define the go-to-market strategy for the products.
  • Devise effective market research and competitive analysis to validate the hypothesis and value of the product being built.
  • Ensure accurate and highly effective PRDs with user stories for the completion of functional requirements.
  • Identify opportunities, set up the roadmap and set the goals for key metrics for the product.
  • Manage the complete product lifecycle, from writing specifications, working across multi-disciplinary teams: design, marketing, and technology to launch core features within our existing ecosystem.
  • Exhibit sound product judgment, ability to formulate product strategy and present clear measurable objectives for yourself and the team that will lead to achieving our business goals.
  • Work with UX design and engineering teams for on time delivery of the product.
  • Work with analytics team to get and understand data to understand feature performance.

Ideal candidate should have the below experience/qualities:

  • Creative thinker with a vision.
  • In-depth knowledge of the Agile process and principles.
  • Sharp analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Previous experience in Marketing, Data is an advantage
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills.
  • Attention to detail.
  • B. Tech, M. Tech/ MBA (preferred)
  • Around 3+ years as Product Management building consumer facing products
